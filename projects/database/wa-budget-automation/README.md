# WA 2025-27 Biennial Operating Budget

## Bill Information

- **Bill Number:** ESSB 5167 (Engrossed Substitute Senate Bill 5167)
- **Title:** Making 2025-2027 fiscal biennium operating appropriations and 2023-2025 fiscal biennium second supplemental operating appropriations
- **Biennium:** 2025-2027
- **Status:** Enacted as Chapter 424, 2025 Laws PV
- **Effective Date:** May 20, 2025 (with partial vetoes by Governor)
- **Budget Size:**
  - General Fund: $77.9 billion
  - Total: $150.4 billion

## Notes

The WA Legislature website blocks automated downloads, so files must be manually downloaded.
Files are large (500-1400 pages typical for operating budget bills) and are excluded from git tracking.
